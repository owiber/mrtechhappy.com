Dissenting Opinion (Unspoken, Unmentioned)

It's there, not to be ignored,
But my mind will run away.
Run away from that thing:
Unspoken, unmentioned...
A scratch at the surface
But can not bring more.
Nothing more; it's too obvious.
There is nothing at all,
Everything is...
Unspoken, unmentioned.
Not a word will flow,
Or even drip; one drop would do.
Or wou--what would it do?
Torment, maim, destroy, reveal?
It is-was-always will be
Unspoken, unmentioned.
