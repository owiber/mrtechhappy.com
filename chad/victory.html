A Bloody Victory

You laugh.
A smile can't help but iron itself to my face.
Whereas few things feel better
Than seeing contentment overwhelm you,
And, thus, overwhelm me.
What fool would steal this joy away from you?
I'm sure any fool would...
Hence, to maintain slight intelligence,
And overall happiness,
I hurriedly and dejectedly
Separate my existence from yours.
