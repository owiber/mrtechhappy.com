Daylight

There goes a little bunny,
Playing with his beloved peers.
Puppies romp through the grass
Tackling and tugging another's ears.

A butterfly floats past my view
It's wings inspiring the cool breeze.
Even the crows are singing a beautiful song
That would bring the scary straw man to his knees.

And as the cute little squirrels
chase one another from limb to limb,
The sun sits upon high and eluminates
All those creatures living below him.
